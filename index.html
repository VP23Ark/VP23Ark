About Us

We are a multi-faceted company with ventures in Tech, Tourism, Media (Galactomedia) and our very own Production House(Galactomedia)

We are a tech company with a vision of sustainable growth and being in harmony with other businesses and environment

We are very keen on building relationships and growth through the following

  • Ark Tech : We are developing new technologies and application to reduce automotive problems with a focus on creating infrastructure that promotes electric vehicles.
  • Ark Tour : We intend to provide the best in class tourism with the utmost priority of vehicular and personal safety of the tourists along with the best views at the most reasonable pricing.
  • Ark Media : Our venture, Galacto Media is a focused group of creative content creators with a range from audio services, video services to awareness campaigns and advertisements catering to any and all kind of businesses or brands.
  • Ark Production : Galactomedia has a very proficient and passionate production team which is ready to provide the greatest possible production value to any content, ad, documentary or any other audio, video, article and photography based service. It is headed by young directors and thoughtful writers.

VP Ark is a service based company that relies on building relationships through providing quality work and being as cost effective as possible.
